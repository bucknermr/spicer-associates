Marcy Buckner, MSW, LICSW a licensed psychotherapist, earned her master's degree from Columbia University Graduate school of Social Work and undergraduate degree from Tufts University. She provides individual, couples, and group psychotherapy to adults and teens, utilizing cognitive behavioral therapy, narrative and dialectical behavioral therapy (DBT) as well as solution-focused and psychodynamic treatment approaches. Her work is based on the belief that individuals have the capacity within themselves to create positive and sustained change in their lives. She has thirty years experience treating mood and anxiety disorders, trauma, grief and loss. In addition, she specializes in emotional aspects of health rehabilitation, including neurological and congenital disabilities, brain injury, cognitive disabilities as well as autism spectrum disorders. Ms. Buckner served as adjunct faculty at University of St. Thomas - St. Catherine's University Graduate School of Social Work in St. Paul, Minnesota, and is a certified clinical supervisor, providing clinical supervision to post graduate clinicians.

Carol. A. Loeffler, Ph.D., earned her doctoral degree in clinical psychology at The University of Toledo, completed her internship at University Hospital in Syracuse, New York, and has been in private practice since that time working with a variety of populations. She has twenty years experience with the geriatric population and individuals of all ages with depression, anxiety, post-traumatic stress disorder, personality disorders and adjustment disorders. In addition to her private practice she has been clinical director of a community mental health agency, taught undergraduate courses at The University of Toledo, served as staff psychologist for several skilled nursing and long term care facilities, and has been selected by her peers to serve on numerous panels and committees on issues such as ethics, death and dying and treatment for victims of sexual abuse. Working with adults of all ages, adolescents and couples, she is accepting new clients at Spicer Associates.
